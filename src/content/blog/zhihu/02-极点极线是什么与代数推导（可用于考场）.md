## 交比与调和点列

要了解极点极线，我们首先回顾什么是交比（可跳过）

定义四点AB;MN的交比为

![ \displaystyle{(AB;MN)=\frac{AM}{MB} / \frac{AN}{NB}} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cdisplaystyle%7B%28AB%3BMN%29%3D%5Cfrac%7BAM%7D%7BMB%7D+%2F+%5Cfrac%7BAN%7D%7BNB%7D%7D+)

其中

![A,B ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%2CB+)

  称为基点偶，

![M,N](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M%2CN)

 称为分点偶。注意这个式子中的比都是有向线段做比，线段同向比为正，反向则为负。

这个式子很容易记忆：从 A 走向 B，途中经历 M，N 点然后做比即可。交比本身有非常多的好的性质，它是射影变换的一个不变量，但是我们今天的主题不涉及这些。

若四点的交比恰好为

![-1](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=-1)

 ，则称这四点成一组调和点列。

若已知三点，要求一点使得这四点为一组调和点列，从式子易知这一点是唯一可被确定的。

对于平面内的的确定一点

![R(x_{0},y_{0})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R%28x_%7B0%7D%2Cy_%7B0%7D%29)

 做**任意直线**

![l](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=l)

 交二次曲线于

![M,N](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M%2CN)

 两点，令

![S(x_{1},y_{1})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S%28x_%7B1%7D%2Cy_%7B1%7D%29)

 ，使得

![(RS;MN)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28RS%3BMN%29)

 为一组调和点列：

## 推导极点极线

我们可以利用 MN 在二次曲线上与交比为 -1 求出 S 的轨迹。注意：以下过程全部为正向推导，实际上在考场上我们知道结论不用这么麻烦，直接把坐标代入即可证明极点极线。

先设出二次曲线

![ C: Ax^2+Bxy+Cy^2+Dx+Ey+F=0 ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+C%3A+Ax%5E2%2BBxy%2BCy%5E2%2BDx%2BEy%2BF%3D0+)

首先，条件告诉我们

![ \begin{align} \frac{RM}{MS} / \frac{RN}{NS} &= -1  \\ \end{align} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cbegin%7Balign%7D+%5Cfrac%7BRM%7D%7BMS%7D+%2F+%5Cfrac%7BRN%7D%7BNS%7D+%26%3D+-1++%5C%5C+%5Cend%7Balign%7D+)

则不妨设

![ \frac{RM}{MS}=-\frac{RN}{NS}=\lambda ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cfrac%7BRM%7D%7BMS%7D%3D-%5Cfrac%7BRN%7D%7BNS%7D%3D%5Clambda+)

即

![ RM=\lambda MS; RN=-\lambda NS ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+RM%3D%5Clambda+MS%3B+RN%3D-%5Clambda+NS+)

又已知

![R(x_{0},y_{0});S(x_{1},y_{1})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R%28x_%7B0%7D%2Cy_%7B0%7D%29%3BS%28x_%7B1%7D%2Cy_%7B1%7D%29)

 与

![R,S,M,N](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R%2CS%2CM%2CN)

 四点共线

不难得到

![M,N](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M%2CN)

 的坐标

![M(x_{M},y_{M});N(x_{N},y_{N})](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M%28x_%7BM%7D%2Cy_%7BM%7D%29%3BN%28x_%7BN%7D%2Cy_%7BN%7D%29)

![ \begin{align} M&=\vec{OM} \\ &=\vec{OR}+\vec{RM} \\ &=\vec{OR}+\frac{\lambda}{1+\lambda}\vec{RS}  \\ &= \vec{OR} + \frac{\lambda}{1+\lambda} (\vec{OS}-\vec{OR}) \\ &=\frac{1}{1+\lambda}\vec{OR}+\frac{\lambda}{1+\lambda} \vec{OS} \\ \\ N&=\dots \\ &=\frac{1}{1-\lambda} \vec{OR} + \frac{-\lambda}{1-\lambda} \vec{OS} \end{align} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cbegin%7Balign%7D+M%26%3D%5Cvec%7BOM%7D+%5C%5C+%26%3D%5Cvec%7BOR%7D%2B%5Cvec%7BRM%7D+%5C%5C+%26%3D%5Cvec%7BOR%7D%2B%5Cfrac%7B%5Clambda%7D%7B1%2B%5Clambda%7D%5Cvec%7BRS%7D++%5C%5C+%26%3D+%5Cvec%7BOR%7D+%2B+%5Cfrac%7B%5Clambda%7D%7B1%2B%5Clambda%7D+%28%5Cvec%7BOS%7D-%5Cvec%7BOR%7D%29+%5C%5C+%26%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B1%2B%5Clambda%7D%5Cvec%7BOR%7D%2B%5Cfrac%7B%5Clambda%7D%7B1%2B%5Clambda%7D+%5Cvec%7BOS%7D+%5C%5C+%5C%5C+N%26%3D%5Cdots+%5C%5C+%26%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B1-%5Clambda%7D+%5Cvec%7BOR%7D+%2B+%5Cfrac%7B-%5Clambda%7D%7B1-%5Clambda%7D+%5Cvec%7BOS%7D+%5Cend%7Balign%7D+)

这个计算过程被有些人称为定比分点定理，在试卷上可以不必写完整过程。结论是当

注意到相减后的方程 **不包含**

![ A x_0 x_1 + B \cdot \frac{x_0 y_1 + x_1 y_0}{2} + C y_0 y_1 + D \cdot \frac{x_0 + x_1}{2} + E \cdot \frac{y_0 + y_1}{2} + F = 0. ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+A+x_0+x_1+%2B+B+%5Ccdot+%5Cfrac%7Bx_0+y_1+%2B+x_1+y_0%7D%7B2%7D+%2B+C+y_0+y_1+%2B+D+%5Ccdot+%5Cfrac%7Bx_0+%2B+x_1%7D%7B2%7D+%2B+E+%5Ccdot+%5Cfrac%7By_0+%2B+y_1%7D%7B2%7D+%2B+F+%3D+0.+)

将

![(x_1, y_1)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28x_1%2C+y_1%29)

 替换为一般点

![(x, y)](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28x%2C+y%29)

 ，即得极点

 的极线方程：

![ \color{blue}{A x_0 x + B \cdot \frac{x_0 y + y_0 x}{2} + C y_0 y + D \cdot \frac{x_0 + x}{2} + E \cdot \frac{y_0 + y}{2} + F = 0} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Ccolor%7Bblue%7D%7BA+x_0+x+%2B+B+%5Ccdot+%5Cfrac%7Bx_0+y+%2B+y_0+x%7D%7B2%7D+%2B+C+y_0+y+%2B+D+%5Ccdot+%5Cfrac%7Bx_0+%2B+x%7D%7B2%7D+%2B+E+%5Ccdot+%5Cfrac%7By_0+%2B+y%7D%7B2%7D+%2B+F+%3D+0%7D+)

注意到这个方程是一次的，因此它表示一条直线。

根据上述式子，易知对任意二次曲线只需做如下替换

![ \begin{align} x^2\to x_{0}x \\ y^2\to y_{0}y \\ x\to \frac{x_{0}+x}{2} \\ y \to \frac{y_{0}+y}{2} \\ xy \to \frac{x_0y+y_{0}x}{2} \end{align} ](https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cbegin%7Balign%7D+x%5E2%5Cto+x_%7B0%7Dx+%5C%5C+y%5E2%5Cto+y_%7B0%7Dy+%5C%5C+x%5Cto+%5Cfrac%7Bx_%7B0%7D%2Bx%7D%7B2%7D+%5C%5C+y+%5Cto+%5Cfrac%7By_%7B0%7D%2By%7D%7B2%7D+%5C%5C+xy+%5Cto+%5Cfrac%7Bx_0y%2By_%7B0%7Dx%7D%7B2%7D+%5Cend%7Balign%7D+)

即可找到某点的极线
