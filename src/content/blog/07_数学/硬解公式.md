#math

对某椭圆或双曲线与某线

$$
\begin{align}
\frac{x^2}{M}+\frac{y^2}{N}&=1 \\
Ax+By+C&=0
\end{align}
$$

有

$$
\begin{align}

x_{1}+x_{2}&=-2\frac{MAC}{K} \\
y_{1}+y_{2}&=-2\frac{NBC}{K} \\
x_{1}x_{2}&=\frac{M(C^2-NB^2)}{K} \\
y_{1}y_{2}&=\frac{N(C^2-MA^2)}{K} \\
x_{1}y_{2}+x_{2}y_{1}&=\frac{2MNAB}{K} \\
\Delta(x)&=4MNB^2 (K - C^2) \\
\Delta(y)&=4MNA^2 (K - C^2) \\
\text{其中} \\
K&=MA^2+NB^2

\end{align}
$$
