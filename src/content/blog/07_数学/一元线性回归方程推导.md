设其为
$$
y=bx+a
$$
记误差为 $Q$ 


$$
\begin{align}
Q(a,b)&=\sum (y_{i}-bx_{i}-a_{i})^2 \\
&=\sum[y_{i}-bx_{i}-(\bar{y}-b\bar{x})+(\bar{y}-b\bar{x})-a]^2 \\
&=\sum[y_{i}-bx_{i}-(\bar{y}-b\bar{x})]^{2} \\
&+2\sum[y_{i}-bx_{i}-(\bar{y}-b\bar{x})][(\bar{y}-b\bar{x})-a]+n[\bar{y}-b\bar{x}-a]^{2} \\
\end{align}
$$

观察第二项

$$
\begin{align}
&2\sum[y_{i}-bx_{i}-(\bar{y}-b\bar{x})][(\bar{y}-b\bar{x})-a] \\
&=2[(\bar{y}-b\bar{x})-a]\sum[y_{i}-\bar{y}+b(\bar{x}-x_{i})] \\
&=2[(\bar{y}-b\bar{x})-a](\sum[y_{i}-\bar{y}+b(\bar{x}-x_{i})]) \\
&=0
\end{align}
$$
