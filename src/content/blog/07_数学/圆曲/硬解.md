根据
$$
\dfrac{x^2}{M}+\dfrac{y^2}{N}=1, Ax+By+C=0
$$
可解出

$$
\begin{align}
&(MA^2+NB^2)x^2+2MACx+M(C^2-NB^2)=0 \\
&(MA^2+NB^2)y^2+2NBCy+N(C^2-MA^2)=0 \\
&x_{1}y_{2}+x_{2}y_{1}=\frac{2MANB}{MA^2+NB^2} \\
&\Delta_{x}=4MNB^2(MA^2+NB^2-C^2) \\
&\Delta_{y}=4MNA^2(MA^2+NB^2-C^2) \\
\end{align}
$$
