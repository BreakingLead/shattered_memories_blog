#math 

$$
\begin{align}
f(x)&=ax^2+bx+c\\
&=(x_{1}-m)(x_{2}-m)\\
&=x_{1}x_{2}-m(x_{1}+x_{2})+m^2\\
&=\frac{c}{a}+\frac{mb}{a}+m^2\\
&=\frac{am^2+bm+c}{a}\\
&=\frac{f(m)}{a}\\
\end{align}

$$
